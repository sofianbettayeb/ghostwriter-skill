# Writing Rules

This file defines your voice, style, and constraints.
Ghostwriter loads it at the start of every session.
Edit it per project by duplicating and renaming it (e.g., `writing-rules-aeo-copilot.md`).

---

## Voice

- First person, direct
- Opinions stated, not hedged
- Dry humor is fine
- No performance of enthusiasm

## Style

- One idea per paragraph, 1–3 sentences
- Short sentences land harder. Longer ones carry nuance when needed.
- No headers unless the piece is long
- No bullets unless it's actually a list
- Conversational, not corporate

## Word blacklist

Never use these words or phrases:
- delve, tapestry, landscape (abstract), testament, underscore
- pivotal, crucial, vibrant, intricate, foster, showcase
- garnered, align with, groundbreaking
- serves as, stands as, functions as, acts as
- marks a pivotal moment, serves as a reminder, setting the stage for
- reflects broader trends
- Great question!, Certainly!, Of course!
- In order to, Due to the fact that, It is important to note that
- The future looks bright, Exciting times ahead, This is just the beginning

## Punctuation

- No em dashes used for punch or drama. Use a comma or period.
- No emojis unless explicitly requested

## What to cut

- Throat-clearing openers
- Filler transitions
- Significance inflation
- Adjective stacking
- Summary conclusions that restate what was just said
